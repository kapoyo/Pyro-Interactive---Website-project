Defend the Net

Defend the Net is an exciting and educational tower defense game where waves of dangerous viruses swarm through a digital pathway, threatening to corrupt your system. Your mission? Claim powerful defense tanks to stop them in their tracks! But there’s a twist. To earn money and unlock tanks, you must correctly answer questions about cybersecurity and online safety. From phishing scams to password protection, your knowledge will be the key to survival. Perfect for students and gamers alike, Defend the Net combines fast-paced action with valuable digital safety lessons in a fun, interactive way. Stay sharp, stay safe, and defend the net!

How To Play

Step 1: Prepare you defence!

• Access the shop to buy tanks.
- Basic Tank ($50): normal firepower.
- Double Tank ($60): faster firing.
• Placement colors guide you: red = invalid (track or overlap).
• Unwanted tanks can be removed using the trash can.

Step 2: Defend against viruses!

• Each wave releases viruses:
• Fast virus = $1
• Normal virus = $2
• Strong virus = $5
• You start with 20 lives; lose one each time a virus escapes.

Step 3: Earn Extra Cash!

• After each wave, answer a cybersecurity question.
• Earn $10–$35 depending on difficulty.

Step 4: Progress Through Waves!

• First 7 waves increase in order of difficulty.
• Later waves are randomized, adding unpredictability.
• Maximize cash, place tanks wisely, and stop the viruses!
