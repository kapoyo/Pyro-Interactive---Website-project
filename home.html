VIRABLOCK

Creator: @zkaam

ViraBlock is a story based RPG that teaches cybersecurity in a unique way. You have been assigned a mission offering a promotion by Coloured Square Headquarters to explore the town of Gizville and educate the citizens about the responsible and safe practices of cybersecurity. Do you have the knowledge to succeed and get promoted, or will you get fired trying?
