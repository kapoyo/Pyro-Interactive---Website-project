The Encrypted Realm

Become a digital detective in The Encrypted Realm, the exciting Scratch game where you learn to defend against cyber threats and cunning schemes for money. Will you fall for the tempting offers, or will your sharp cybersecurity skills expose the truth? Navigate tricky scenarios mimicking real-world dangers, where every decision impacts your safety. Discover how tiny personal "tips" can put accounts at risk, teaching you vital lessons about phishing and social engineering. This game is your training ground for digital defense. Play now and secure your future online!

How To Play

This is a User Guide on the Encrypted Realm.

Click the green flag to begin the game. Then you will first be led into a Main Menu with a play button, instructions button and accessibility button.

The instructions will tell you the aim of the game and what you should do. This game is 2D, so walking around will just be limited to left, right, up and down movements. To navigate your character, you can use the WASD keys (W for upwards, A for left, S for downwards and D for right) or the arrow keys.

You need to go around the map talking to characters, and those characters will present you with questions and it is up to you to determine if you can make money or if you are being scammed online. You need to make as much money as possible. A message will appear if you are within talking range of an NPC, and you will be able to interact with them. Sometimes they will ask questions, and to close the question (if you got it right or wrong) you have to get out of talking distance and the question will automatically disappear.
