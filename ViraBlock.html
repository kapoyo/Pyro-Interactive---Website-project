VIRABLOCK

ViraBlock is a story based RPG that teaches cybersecurity in a unique way. You have been assigned a mission offering a promotion by Coloured Square Headquarters to explore the town of Gizville and educate the citizens about the responsible and safe practices of cybersecurity. Do you have the knowledge to succeed and get promoted, or will you get fired trying?

How To Play

Starting Menu

Press the Green Flag to start the game, press the stop button to STOP THE GAME, press the green flag again to restart the game.
Press the start button to continue the game.

Gameplay

This game primarily uses [WASD] keys with [W] being up, [S] down, [D] right and [A] left.
For dialogue, press [SPACE] to proceed dialogue when dialogue is finished and press [SPACE] to skip dialogue. Press [W] or [S] when given a question to switch your response and press [SPACE] to select the option. Make sure to be nice to citizens so you can gain that yummy charisma.

Your mission is to help the citizens of GIZVILLE get educated on cyber security. To do this, choose the correct dialogue options for them to become educated on cybersecurity and this tests your knowledge.

Going from Screen to Screen

You can travel to other edges of the map to go to new map

Win Condition

You win the game after helping all the NPCs with their cybersecurity problem.
